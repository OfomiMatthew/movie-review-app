Welcome to the Movie App

Discover movies, get detailed information, and share your reviews!

Our Features

  • Search for movies by title and year.
  • View detailed information about each movie.
  • Read and write reviews for your favorite movies.

How It Works

  • Search Movies: Use the search bar on the homepage to find movies by title and optionally filter by year.
  • Movie Details: Click on a movie to view detailed information, including the plot, actors, and more.
  • Reviews: Logged-in users can write reviews for movies and also edit their own reviews.

Getting Started

To use all features of the app, please log in. If you don't have an account, you can create one by signing up on the login page.
